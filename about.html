About Me

I grew up in a small farming community which provided me with a unique set of life lessons and character traits that I have always tried to carry with me. The two most important qualities- the pillars I have allowed to guide and drive my own life- are loyalty and striving to improve the lives of others.

When I left home and joined the military I saw these familiar qualities in the people around me. In my comrades, these traits were even stronger than I’d seen back home. Seeing such loyalty and concern for others all around me was a real turning point, and I knew that when I returned home it was going to be important to keep these pillars central to my life. When the time came for me to decide how I was going to do this, helpful financial advice was an obvious choice.

I didn’t have a lot of financial guidance growing up so I had to teach myself about financial responsibility. Picking up these skills had a dramatic effect on my personal life. I knew that sharing this knowledge and skill set was just the way to improve the lives of others that I was looking for.

When I started working in the financial industry- gaining more expertise in financial planning and retirement savings- I realized that loyalty and a commitment to others wasn’t a core value of many larger firms. I knew that if I wanted those qualities to be at the forefront of the financial advice that I was giving, I would have to set out on my own.

That’s why I founded Money By Pounds- to give people, especially those who are just starting out on their savings endeavor- a partner they could trust on their journey towards financial stability.

When it comes to personal financial security, it’s important to know that you have a partner who values your life and goals, and is not afraid to showcase their loyalty to you. That’s just what you’ll have with Money By Pounds- a loyal partner working to improve your life and the lives of others.
