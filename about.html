About Me

I wasn't supposed to be a financial planner.

I grew up in a small farming community in rural Washington. As a child, farming was all I knew. It taught me loyalty, hard work and the power of service to others.

These values bind together and strengthen small communities like mine.

When I joined the military, I was happy to find a new community that valued loyalty and service to others in the people around me. I knew that when I returned home and started my family, it was going to be important to keep these pillars central to my life.

I didn’t have a lot of financial guidance growing up.

I had to teach myself about financial responsibility. Establishing financial stability wasn't easy, but it had a dramatic effect on my life. I have been able to lead my family and children better because of it, and that's the most important thing.

I came to realize that the knowledge and discipline I had achieved could serve others around me and across the world.

I created Money By Pounds in order to give people, especially those just starting out, a partner they could trust, and a coach for their journey to financial stability.

When it comes to personal finances, it’s important to know that you have a partner who shares your values, your life, who you can trust to guide you toward your goals.

I’m Richard Pounds, and I’ll work hard to help you reach your financial goals.
