About Me

I wasn't supposed to be a financial planner.

I grew up in a small farming community in rural Washington. As a child, farming was all I knew. It taught me loyalty and the power of service to others.

These values bind together and strengthen small communities like mine.

When I joined the military, I was happy to find a new community that valued loyalty and service to others in the people around me. I knew that when I returned home, it was going to be important to keep these pillars central to my life.

I didn’t have a lot of financial guidance growing up.
I had to teach myself about financial responsibility. Establishing financial stability wasn't easy, but it had a dramatic effect on my life.

I came to realize that the knowledge and discipline I had achieved could serve my community.

I created Money By Pounds in order to give people, especially those just starting out, a partner they could trust.

When it comes to personal finances, it’s important to know that you have a partner who shares your values your life, who you can trust to guide you toward your goals.

That’s just what you’ll have with Money By Pounds--a loyal partner working to improve your life and the lives of others.
